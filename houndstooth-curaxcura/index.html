The architectural motifs nod to TCM as the foundational architecture of wellbeing, time-tested systems that structure and support vitality. The final Cura X Cura logo consolidates a shooting star-like form in an abstract ‘X’, representing a moment of alignment and release. In the context of Traditional Chinese Medicine, this reflects the instant when qi begins to flow freely, restoring balance after stagnation.

When combined with a traditional Chinese architectural motif, the shooting-star feels grounded rather than cosmic — suggesting breakthrough within structure, and progress that is rooted in tradition rather than detached from it.
